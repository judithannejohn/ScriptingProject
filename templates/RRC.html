Robotics Research Center

The IIIT Robotics Research Lab is a part of International Institute of Information Technonology at Hyderabad, India. The lab aims to work on research problems and innovative projects that extend the state of the art in robotics. The centre's research focuses in the areas of Mobile and Aerial Robotics, Robotic Vision, Mechanism Design and Multi Robotic Systems. The lab is well equipped with robot platforms and advanced sensors and has been publishing in top international conferences like ICRA, IROS, IJCAI, AAMAS.
