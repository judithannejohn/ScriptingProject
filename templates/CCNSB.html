CCNSB: Center for Computational Natural Sciences and Bioinformatics (CCNSB)

About CCNSB: Center for Computational Natural Sciences and Bioinformatics (CCNSB) is a research center in IIIT Hyderabad that is involved in teaching and research in the areas of computational chemistry, computational and systems biology, bioinformatics, bio-molecular simulations, nanosciences, statistical mechanics and complex systems.
